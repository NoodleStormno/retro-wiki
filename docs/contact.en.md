# Contact Us

We welcome your feedback, questions, and suggestions. Here are the best ways to get in touch with us.

## Communication Channels

### GitHub Issues
For technical issues, content corrections, or feature requests:
- **Repository**: [https://github.com/NoodleStormno/retro-wiki](https://github.com/NoodleStormno/retro-wiki)
- **Issue Templates**: Use our pre-defined templates for bug reports and feature requests
- **Response Time**: Typically within 48 hours for technical issues

### Community Forum
Join our community discussions:
- **Platform**: Discord (coming soon)
- **Purpose**: Share repair experiences, ask questions, connect with other enthusiasts
- **Rules**: Be respectful and follow community guidelines

### Email Contact
For formal inquiries, partnerships, or media requests:
- **General Inquiries**: info@retroconsolewiki.org
- **Technical Support**: support@retroconsolewiki.org
- **Partnerships**: partnerships@retroconsolewiki.org

## What We Can Help With

### Technical Support
- Clarification of repair steps
- Troubleshooting specific issues
- Tool and material recommendations
- Safety concerns

### Content Contributions
- Submitting your own repair guides
- Reporting errors or outdated information
- Suggesting new console models to cover
- Providing translation assistance

### General Inquiries
- Website functionality questions
- Usage permissions and licensing
- Collaboration opportunities
- Media and press inquiries

## Response Times

- **Urgent Technical Issues**: 24-48 hours
- **General Questions**: 2-3 business days
- **Content Contributions**: 1 week for review
- **Partnership Inquiries**: 1-2 weeks

## Before Contacting Us

To help us serve you better:

1. **Check Existing Content**: Search our knowledge base first
2. **Be Specific**: Include console model, symptoms, and what you've tried
3. **Provide Details**: Photos/videos help diagnose issues
4. **Reference Guides**: Mention which guide you're following

## Community Guidelines

When interacting with our team or community:

- **Be Respectful**: Everyone is here to learn and help
- **Share Knowledge**: Your experiences help others
- **Stay Safe**: Never attempt repairs beyond your skill level
- **Credit Sources**: Respect intellectual property

## Privacy Note

We respect your privacy. Personal information shared via contact forms or email will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties without your consent. For more details, see our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md).
