# About Us

## Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving the legacy of classic game consoles by providing comprehensive, accurate, and accessible repair guides. Our goal is to help enthusiasts around the world keep their childhood memories alive and functional.

## Who We Are

We are a community of retro gaming enthusiasts, professional repair technicians, and preservationists who share a passion for classic gaming hardware. Our team includes:

- **Veteran technicians** with 20+ years of experience in Akihabara repair shops
- **Hardware engineers** specializing in vintage electronics
- **Community moderators** who curate and verify repair information
- **Content creators** who document repair processes in detail

## What We Do

1. **Create Detailed Repair Guides**: Step-by-step instructions with photos and videos
2. **Maintain Knowledge Base**: Continuously update information based on community feedback
3. **Source Replacement Parts**: Information on where to find authentic and compatible parts
4. **Build Community**: Connect repair enthusiasts worldwide
5. **Preserve History**: Document repair techniques that might otherwise be lost

## Our Values

- **Accuracy**: Every guide is verified by multiple experienced technicians
- **Accessibility**: Guides are written for different skill levels
- **Safety**: Emphasize proper safety procedures in all repairs
- **Sustainability**: Promote repair over replacement to reduce electronic waste
- **Community**: Knowledge sharing and mutual support
