# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: March 1, 2026*

## Introduction

Welcome to Retro Console Repair Guide. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Contact Information**: When you email us or submit forms
- **Technical Questions**: Details about repair issues you're experiencing
- **Feedback and Suggestions**: Content you submit for improvement
- **Community Contributions**: Repair guides or experiences you share

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, navigation patterns
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, device information, IP address
- **Cookies**: For site functionality and analytics (see Cookie Policy below)

### Information from Third Parties
- **GitHub**: If you interact with our repository
- **Analytics Services**: Aggregated usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Provide Services**: Answer technical questions, improve repair guides
- **Website Operation**: Maintain and improve website functionality
- **Communication**: Respond to inquiries and provide updates
- **Community Building**: Facilitate knowledge sharing among enthusiasts

### Analytical Uses
- **Improve Content**: Understand which guides are most helpful
- **Technical Optimization**: Identify and fix website issues
- **User Experience**: Make the site more accessible and useful

### Legal Basis for Processing
- **Consent**: When you voluntarily provide information
- **Legitimate Interest**: For website operation and improvement
- **Legal Obligation**: When required by law

## Information Sharing

### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing Circumstances
We may share information only in these situations:
- **Service Providers**: With trusted partners who help operate our website
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Safety Emergencies**: To prevent harm to individuals
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger or acquisition

### Anonymized Data
We may share aggregated, anonymized data for:
- **Research**: Academic or industry research on retro gaming
- **Statistics**: Public reports on repair trends
- **Improvement**: Working with partners to improve repair resources

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- **Encryption**: SSL/TLS for data transmission
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and updates
- **Employee Training**: Privacy and security awareness

### Data Retention
We retain information only as long as necessary:
- **Contact Information**: Until inquiry is resolved plus 1 year
- **Technical Data**: Aggregated after 2 years
- **Backup Data**: Securely deleted after 30 days

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request copies of your personal information
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Restriction**: Request limitation of processing
- **Objection**: Object to certain types of processing
- **Portability**: Request transfer of your data

### How to Exercise Rights
Contact us at: privacy@retroconsolewiki.org
We will respond within 30 days.
