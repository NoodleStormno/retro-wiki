## Repair Steps

### 1. Safety Preparation & Battery Removal
1. **Complete shutdown**: Ensure PSP is fully powered down, not in standby
2. **Remove UMD disc**: Open UMD compartment, remove disc
3. **Open battery compartment cover**: Slide battery cover lock, open cover
4. **Remove old battery**: Gently pull out battery, don't use excessive force
5. **Disconnect**: Battery connects via metal contactsâ€”simply pull out

### 2. New Battery Installation
1. **Inspect new battery**: Confirm battery model is PSP-110 (specific to PSP 1000)
2. **Clean contacts**: Clean metal contacts in battery compartment with isopropyl alcohol
3. **Insert new battery**: Place new battery with metal contacts facing down, gently push in
4. **Confirm seating**: Listen for slight "click" indicating proper installation
5. **Close cover**: Slide battery cover lock, ensure cover is secure

### 3. Initial Charging & Calibration
1. **Connect charger**: Use original PSP charger (5V 2A)
2. **Charging indicator**: Orange light indicates charging
3. **Full charge**: Charge for about 3-4 hours until orange light turns off
4. **Power on test**: Unplug charger, power on to test battery life
5. **Battery calibration**:
   - After full charge, use normally until automatic shutdown
   - Fully charge again, repeat 2-3 times to complete calibration

### 4. Common Issues & Solutions
#### Issue 1: New Battery Won't Charge
1. Check charger: Confirm charger outputs 5V 2A
2. Check charging port: Clean PSP charging port
3. Check battery contacts: Ensure contacts are clean and making good contact
4. Try different charger: Rule out charger failure

#### Issue 2: Short Battery Life
1. **Screen brightness**: Reduce brightness (default maximum is very power-hungry)
2. **Wireless functions**: Turn off WiFi if not using
3. **UMD drive**: Reduce UMD disc usage (drive consumes significant power)
4. **CPU frequency**: Use default 333MHz for games, don't overclock

#### Issue 3: Loose Battery Compartment
1. Check battery compartment clips: Look for damage
2. Check battery dimensions: Ensure new battery is correct size
3. Temporary fix: Add small piece of double-sided tape to back of battery to increase thickness
